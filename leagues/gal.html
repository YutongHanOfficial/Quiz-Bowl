Genesee Academic League (GAL)


Purpose

The Genesee Academic League involves high school students in a rapid recall academic competition that is designed to showcase students' knowledge and wisdom.


Competition Dates

All league meets will occur on Wednesday afternoons. Each team will play every other team in their division once during the season. At the final meet, teams will play a crossover game against a team of the same ranking from the other division.

To see specific dates and matches, view the league schedule for the current school year.
